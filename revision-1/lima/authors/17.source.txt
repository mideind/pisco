How do you learn to play the guitar?
<|system|>
There are several ways to learn how to play the guitar, including: 
* Taking lessons from a teacher who can help you learn and give personalized tips based on your level.
* Using online videos and tutorials that offer free lessons and exercises for beginners.
* Practicing independently using books or websites that teach basic techniques and songs.

It is important to remember that learning to play an instrument takes time and practice, so be patient with yourself and don't get discouraged if you don't see progress right away.
